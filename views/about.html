Who is this guy?

Hello! I'm Jacob Martin, a multi-disciplinary creative from Atlanta. I currently freelance as a designer for local Atlanta bands and artists, and I design and develop websites as well.

I bring an open mind, open heart, and open ears to my design approach in order to reach many perspectives to find more and better solutions. I’m passionate about listening, observing, and learning as these qualities are foundational to good communication, and therefore, good design.

In my free time I enjoy rebuilding and riding UJM bikes, playing my guitar, and smoking meats on the grill. Thank you for taking the time to visit my site and please reach out if I can help you find creative solutions you're looking for.
