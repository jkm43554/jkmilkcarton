the corduroy blue
art direction / illustration

The Corduroy Blue (TCB) is a power pop band based in Atl, Ga. TCB aims to blend the charisma of 60s/70s pop with the charm of 90s Britpop; all in with a modern context.

TCB has gained cult following among the undeground Atlanta rock scene for the atmosphere created at their shows, and the connections they make with their fans and other local bands.

The merch and posters were designed to best capture this atmosphere and energy one would experience when listening to their music or watching their shows; while also making references that longtime fans and members of the scene will know and enjoy.

gradient
art direction

Life is a gradient. It contrasts smoothly through highs and lows to provide an experience like no other. To ignore the lows would be a disservice as you would never be able to fully appreciate the highs; but to only dwell in the lows can keep you from moving forward.

Gradient is a music festival celebrating the highs and lows of life. It is a one-night-only concert highlighting artists who contemplate on the dark moments of life, but manage to keep hopeful undertones.

This gradient-smothered identity is inspired by the artists' abilities to be a source of light while surrounded in darkness.
